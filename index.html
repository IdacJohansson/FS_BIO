The Chronicles of Narnia: The Voyage of the Dawn Treader follows Lucy and Edmund Pevensie as they return to Narnia with their cousin Eustace and join King Caspian aboard the majestic ship The Dawn Treader. Their quest leads them across mysterious islands, dangerous seas, and encounters with magical creatures as they seek to rescue kidnapped lords and confront a rising evil threatening Narnia.

The film was directed by Michael Apted and stars Georgie Henley, Skandar Keynes, Ben Barnes, and Will Poulter.

It premiered in 2010, with the world premiere on November 30, 2010, and the general release beginning in early December of the same year.
